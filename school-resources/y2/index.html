Math

MATH 2Z03 Formula Sheet/Study Guide

You know my MO already when it comes to writing these things. TeX, markdown source, pdf. For the 2Z03 final exam, you are permitted two two-sided crib sheets. I have thus two versions: a nice and comfortable full-length one with big equations (the Study Guide), and a compressed one that fits right in 4 pages (the Formula Sheet), using margins, scaling, and some formula tricks, and a full rewrite to get it down.

However, the Formula Sheet is not completely done. I have listed some more things I suggest you add, at the end of the Sheet. Since markdown is quite limited when it comes to space compression, I wasn't able to add them myself. I suggest you google these yourself and write them on the printout, squeezing them in in the gaps. Or don't, if you were 1ZB3's strongest warrior. Your choice.
